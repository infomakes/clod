Home Automation

The term "home automation" refers to the automatic and electronic control of features, activities, and appliances in the home. In layman's terms, it means you can easily control your home's utilities and features via the Internet, making life more convenient and secure while also spending less on household bills.

Home automation is a network of hardware, communication, and electronic interfaces that work together to connect everyday devices to the Internet.

Each device has sensors and is WiFi-connected, allowing you to manage them from your smartphone or tablet whether you're at home or thousands of miles away. This enables you to turn on the lights, lock the front door, or even turn down the heat from anywhere.
